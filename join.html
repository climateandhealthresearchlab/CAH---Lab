Why Join the Climate and Health Research Lab?

The Climate and Health Research Lab focuses on interdisciplinary research at the intersection of climate science, public health, data science, and environmental sustainability.

  • Hands-on research with real-world climate and health datasets
  • Training in Python, remote sensing, and statistical modelling
  • Opportunities for publications and conference presentations
  • Supportive and collaborative research environment

Who Can Apply?

We welcome applications from:

  • Undergraduate students (Level 200 and above)
  • MSc and PhD students
  • Early-career researchers
  • Students with backgrounds in Meteorology, Climate Science, Physics, Environmental Science, Computer Science, or related fields

Available Positions

  • Undergraduate Research Assistants
  • Graduate Research Assistants
  • Data Science & Machine Learning Interns
  • Visiting Researchers & Collaborators

How to Apply

  • Brief motivation statement (1 page)
