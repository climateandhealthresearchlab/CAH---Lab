Welcome to the Climate and Health Lab! We are led by Dr. Yamba at the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST).

Our research explores the intersection of climate variability, environmental health, and sustainable development.

We use climate data analysis, satellite remote sensing, and modeling techniques to understand how climate extremes such as heatwaves and floods affect public health and ecosystems.
